About

Hi, I'm {{ site.name }}. I research intelligent agents — how they plan, reason, use tools, and reflect on their own thinking. I'm especially interested in building agents that are transparent about how they reach their conclusions, not just what they conclude.

This site is where I collect the things I'm building and the things I'm thinking about. The Projects section links to my open-source work, and Articles is an evolving set of notes and essays on agent reasoning.
